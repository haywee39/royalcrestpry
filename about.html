HISTORY HIGHTLIGHTS

Mrs Ojo’s goal was to bring about a good learning environment for children in the rural area she lived in giving them equal chance at competing with their peers by affordable education for the same quality.
As Mrs Ojo once said “I want to raise children with big Hearts, innovative Hands and bright Heads.”

2011

At the age of Thirty Four,
she opens the nursery and primary school, Royal Crest School, with 3 students.

2017

There was a growing trend of a relapse due to graduating students not having a better environment to continue with the same quality that had been during their early education stages

2020

The Royal Crest Schools was established with Mrs Ilegbodo appointed as the Principal who is a seasoned educator with over 25 years of experience.

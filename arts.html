ARTS PROGRAM

We offer creative programs as it’s the cornerstone in our approach to help learners explore their
artsy and innovative abilities.
From performing arts to visual arts there are opportunities for every passion.

Chorus/Choir

Learners explore vocal performance through group singing, learning harmony, technique, and stage presence.
Performances build confidence and a love for music.

Band

Through instrumental training and ensemble work, learners develop musical skills, teamwork, and expression while performing at school events and concerts.

Theatre

Our theatre program builds confidence and creativity as learners engage in acting, stagecraft, and live performances, bringing stories to life on stage.

Fashion Design

Learners express their style through sketching, sewing, and design. The program fosters creativity, craftsmanship, and a strong fashion sense.

Photography

Learners learn to capture moments through the lens, mastering composition, lighting, and editing while developing their unique visual voice

Content Creation/ Videography

From filming to editing, learners create original digital content, learning storytelling, production, and media skills for today's digital world.

Visual/ Creative Arts

Through drawing, painting, and mixed media, learners explore artistic techniques and express ideas creatively, with their work showcased in exhibitions.

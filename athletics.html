ATHLETICS

At Royal Crest, sports play a key role in developing teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness. Our athletics program includes a range of competitive and recreational activities

Basketball

Learners build agility, strategy, and teamwork through practice and competition on the court.

Chess

Chess sharpens focus and critical thinking, encouraging strategic play and mental discipline.

Football

Our football teams emphasize skill, coordination, and team spirit , competing with energy and pride.

Track & Field

From sprints to long-distance runs, students train for speed, endurance, and personal excellence.

Javelin

Learners learn the technique and power behind this classic field event, building strength and precision.

Shot Put

With focus on strength and form, learners compete in this powerful throwing event.

Interhouse Sports

Held twice a year, our Interhouse Sports competitions brings the whole school together in a spirited celebration of athletic talent and house pride.
